My Pikatastic Website!!!

Pikachu is one of the most beloved creatures in the Pokemon universe. Possessing, many traits that make him seem almost human-like, he feels like a bridge of sorts between the more scary and almost alien Pokemon and the humans who train them, making them seem more understandable through his loveability. Pikachu is:

  • Friendly
  • Loyal
  • Kind
  • Brave
  • Strong

Pikachu, in short, is everything we want in a human friend. But, he's a Pokemon!!! Poke that cheeky Pikachu anywhere for more info.
